You'll need an App Password. Bluesky lets you create one without sharing your real password.
  1. Open bsky.app/settings/app-passwords in another tab.
  2. Click Add app password, name it "Discord roles" or similar.
  3. Copy the 4-group password Bluesky shows (looks like abcd-efgh-ijkl-mnop).
  4. Paste it below. We use it just once to confirm the handle is yours, then discard it.
What does this app give access to?

Nothing on your side. We only read your public profile and confirm the handle belongs to you — we never see your real password, never post on your behalf, and don't store the app password. You can revoke it on the same Bluesky settings page anytime.
